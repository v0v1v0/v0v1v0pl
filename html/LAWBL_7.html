sim18ccfa40 R Documentation

Simulated CCFA data with LI and missingness

Description

Categorical CFA data simulated based on 18 items, 3 factors, and 4 categories with local independence and 10 percent missingness at random; factorial correlation \Phi=.3.

Usage

sim18ccfa40

Format

A list with components:

dat

A dataset with simulated responses of 1000 individuals to 18 items

qlam

Loading pattern and values used to simulated the data
