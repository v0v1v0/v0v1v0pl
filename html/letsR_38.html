temp R Documentation

Avarege temperature raster for the world.

Description

Average temperature raster in Celsius degrees(multiplied by 100) for the world in 10 arc min of resolution. Data was modfied from WorldClim (https://worldclim.com/, downloaded in 05/2014).

Usage

data(temp)

Source

Hijmans, R.J., S.E. Cameron, J.L. Parra, P.G. Jones and A. Jarvis, 2005. Very high resolution interpolated climate surfaces for global land areas. International Journal of Climatology 25: 1965-1978.
