Phyllomedusa R Documentation

Geographic distribution of Phyllomedusa genus

Description

Geographic distribution of the Southern American frog genera Phyllomedusa. Data was modfied from IUCN (https://www.iucnredlist.org/, downloaded in 05/2014). There are 32 species and 46 polygons.

Usage

data(Phyllomedusa)

Source

IUCN - https://www.iucnredlist.org/. 2014.
