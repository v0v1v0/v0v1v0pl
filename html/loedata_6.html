Firmdata R Documentation

Open DART firm data

Description

Korean firm data for 2018 in KOSPI and KOSDAQ

Usage

data(Firmdata)

Format

A data frame with 2073 rows and 24 variables:

corpcode

Firm code

market

"KOSPI" or "KOSDAQ"

kospi

=1 if KOSPI

kosdaq

=1 if KOSDAQ

indcode

industry code

sic0

one of A, C, GHI, DEF, JK, and Others

sic1

A, B, ..., U (top SIC categories)

sic2

2-digit SIC

sic3

3-digit SIC

estdate

establishment date in yyyymmdd

estyear

establishment year

age

=2018-estyear

inkorea

=1 if the firm operates in Korea

status

="000" if firm information is available

nemp

number of employees

totsal

total annual salary paid (sum)

avgten

average tenure in years

avgsal

=totsal/nemp

fstype

CFS or OFS

accstatus

="000" if account information is available

sales

sales in KRW

oprofit

operating profit in KRW

netinc

net income in KRW

Author(s)

NA

Source
