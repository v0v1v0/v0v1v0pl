amarilloprecip R Documentation

Annual Maximum Precipitation Data for Amarillo, Texas

Description

Annual maximum precipitation data for Amarillo, Texas

Usage

data(amarilloprecip)

Format

An R data.frame with

YEAR

The calendar year of the annual maxima.

DEPTH

The depth of 7-day annual maxima rainfall in inches.
